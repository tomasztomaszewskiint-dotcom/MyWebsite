File No. 003 — Engagement Process

How an engagement actually runs, start to close.

Four stages, always in this order — because skipping ahead is exactly how compliance gaps get missed in the first place.

  • STAGE 01

    Discovery Audit

    Two to four days on-site, walking the floor with your team rather than just reviewing paperwork. We map current practice against every standard that actually applies to your facility and output a ranked findings report — no boilerplate checklists.

  • STAGE 02

    Remediation Plan

    Each finding gets a fix, an owner, and a realistic date — sequenced around your actual production schedule, not an idealized one. You get a single working document your team can act on immediately.

  • STAGE 03

    Implementation Support

    We stay engaged while fixes go in — answering questions, adjusting the plan when reality intervenes, and verifying each item as it closes rather than waiting until the end to check.

  • STAGE 04

    Ongoing Oversight

    Quarterly check-ins that catch drift before it becomes a finding — regulatory changes, staff turnover, and process shortcuts all get reviewed on a fixed cadence, so your next inspection is a formality.

Most engagements begin with a Discovery Audit.
