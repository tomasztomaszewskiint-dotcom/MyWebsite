File No. 002 — Firm History

Started by two auditors who got tired of watching good fixes arrive too late.

Ledger & Vale was founded in 2014 after a decade spent on the other side of the table — inside compliance and safety teams at mid-size manufacturers. We built the firm we wished we'd had.

Our approach

We work from the floor up, not the binder down.

Most compliance work fails for the same reason: it's written for the auditor, not for the people who have to live with it every shift. Every engagement starts on-site, with the people actually running the line — because the gap between policy and practice is where the real risk lives.

We keep engagements small and senior. No junior analysts learning on your file, no generic templates lightly reskinned. The person who walks your facility is the person who writes your report.
